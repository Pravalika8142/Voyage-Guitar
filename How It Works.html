How It Works

The innovative folding guitar mechanism explained

Discover the Magic of Portability

Our patented hinge design lets you fold your guitar in seconds, without compromising on tone or durability. Experience unmatched convenience, whether you're heading to a gig or exploring the world, our guitars travel effortlessly with you.

How It Works

Step 1: Unlock the Hinge

Simply unlock the hinge located at the neck of the guitar using our custom mechanism.

Step 2: Fold the Neck

Gently fold the neck downwards, reducing the guitar's size by nearly 50%.

Step 3: Pack and Go

Place your folded guitar in its specially designed travel case and you're ready to hit the road!
